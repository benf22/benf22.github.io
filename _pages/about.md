I am a PhD candidate in the Computer Science department at Bar Ilan University, supervised by [Prof. Gal Chechik](https://chechiklab.biu.ac.il/~gal/) and [Dr. Idan Schwartz](https://idansc.github.io/). My research focuses on understanding Deep Neural Networks, with a particular emphasis on Multi-Modality and Disentanglement in Generative Networks.

I hold an M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Tel Aviv University, where I researched Speech, specifically Prosody (intonation, rhythm, timbre, etc.). I also have a B.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from Tel Aviv University, where my research centered on detecting human gait and movement using Sonar.

Prior to my PhD studies, I served as the Director of AI & Algorithms at Microsoft, leading the Data Science discipline in the Surface and WSSI groups. My team worked on computer vision, securing LLMs and compression of DNNs. Before that, I was a Senior Data Scientist in Microsoft's Video Indexer group, focusing on developing Deep Learning models for audio and video modalities in the media and autonomous driving domains. I also worked at Mobileye as an Algorithms Developer and Team Leader, specializing in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, signal fusion, and temporal analysis.

I am passionate about understanding images, videos, music, and speech.
