I am a Ph.D candidate in the Computer Science department at Bar Ilan University, supervised by [Prof. Gal Chechik](https://chechiklab.biu.ac.il/~gal/) and [Dr. Idan Schwartz](https://idansc.github.io/). My research focuses on understanding Deep Neural Networks, with a particular emphasis on Multi-Modality and Disentanglement in Generative Networks.

Prior to my Ph.D studies, I served as the Director of AI & Algorithms at Microsoft, leading the Data Science discipline in the [Surface](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/surface) and [WSSI](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/copilot-plus-pcs?r=1) groups. My team worked on Computer Vision, securing LLMs and DNNs compression. Before that, I was a Senior Data Scientist in Microsoft's [Video Indexer](https://vi.microsoft.com/en-us) group, focusing on developing Deep Learning models for audio and video modalities in the media and autonomous driving domains. I also worked at Mobileye as an Algorithms Developer and Team Leader, specializing in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Signal Fusion, and Temporal Analysis.

I hold an M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Tel Aviv University, where I researched Speech, specifically [Prosody](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prosody_(linguistics)) (intonation, rhythm, timbre, etc.). I also have a B.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from Tel Aviv University, where my research centered on detecting human gait and movement using Sonar.

I am passionate about understanding images, videos, music, and speech.
